What is ADOBO?

Adobo or adobar is the immersion of cooked food in a stock composed variously of paprika, oregano, salt, garlic, and vinegar to preserve and enhance its flavor. The Portuguese variant is known as Carne de vinha d'alhos.

Cooking some ADOBO

Ingredients

1/4 cup soy sauce, 1 cup vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, Pinch of salt and peppercorn, 3 bay leaves, 1 cup water, 1 medium onion, chopped, 4 cloves of garlic, pressed or chopped, Cooking oil, Salt and pepper to taste


    Ⅰ

  1. Add cooking oil to the pan
  2. Cook the chopped garlic
  3. Add the onions if the garlic is cooked lightly brown

    ⅠⅠ

  1. Add your chicken
  2. Cook your chicken for 5 mins

    ⅠⅠⅠ

  1. Add the soy, vinegar, peppercorn, salt, sugar, water, and bay leaves
  2. Then stir and make sure it is mixed

    Ⅳ

  1. Wait for your chicken to a boil
  2. If it boils turn your stove to low heat
  3. Add water if it is too dry

    Ⅴ

  1. Let the chicken simmer for abour 20-30 minutes
  2. Make sure that you cover your pan
  3. Let it simmer until the chicken is tender

    Ⅵ

  1. Taste your ADOBO
  2. If salty add water
  3. If not and it taste good then Serve
